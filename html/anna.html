Anna Morosini was born in 1987 in Foligno, italy.

She graduated in Modern Literature, and holds a Master in High Level Contemporary Image at Fondazione Fotografia of Modena.

She started to photograph when she was a child and she mainly works on portraits and body language.

She made exhibitions across Italy and Europe and besides artistic projects, she used to work in the fashion industry, with editorials, advertising campaigns and music video clips.
