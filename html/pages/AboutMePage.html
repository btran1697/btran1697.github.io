Bao-Anh Tran is a student at the University of Connecticut at Stamford studying Digital Media and Design with a concentration in Web Design. After years of studying biology and chemistry in hopes of pursuing a medical career, she realized her true passion and heart for the arts. She enjoys photography as much as she does drawing, and is continuing to explore art mediums outside her comfort zone. She plans to one day work as a web or graphic designer for a small company in a big city.
